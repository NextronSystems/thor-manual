THOR Scanner User Manual
=======================================

In this manual you can find all the information regarding THOR.
This version of the manual focuses on our old version of THOR,
which is version **10.6**. If you are looking for information
on the newest version, **10.7**, you can have a look `here <https://thor-manual.nextron-systems.com>`_.
